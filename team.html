Home / Meet the Team

The people behind the support.

A small, dedicated team of housing and support professionals. To protect the privacy and safety of our residents and staff, we introduce our team by role rather than by photograph.

Our team

Experienced, dedicated, and genuinely invested.

Every resident is supported by a team that understands supported housing — and understands people.

R
Leadership

Registered Manager

Oversees the service, safeguarding, and standards — making sure every resident receives safe, high-quality support.

S
Frontline

Support Workers

Your dedicated day-to-day support. One consistent person who knows your situation and advocates for you.

H
Specialist

Housing & Benefits Lead

Handles tenancies, Housing Benefit and Universal Credit — making sure the practical side is properly sorted.

W
Wellbeing

Wellbeing & Recovery Support

Trauma-informed support for mental health and addiction recovery, linked to local specialist services.

O
Around the clock

On-Call Team

The people who answer when it matters most — providing 24/7 emergency out-of-hours cover, every day of the year.

M
Move-on

Move-On Coordinator

Plans the route towards independent living, with the right timing and support to make the transition stick.

A note on privacy. We choose not to publish staff photographs. Many of the people we support are vulnerable, and keeping our team's identities discreet is part of how we keep everyone safe.
