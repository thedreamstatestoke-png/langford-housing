Frequently asked

Questions landlords always ask us.

How is this different from a regular letting agent?
A letting agent still leaves you exposed to voids, arrears, and management headaches. We sign a commercial lease with you directly — making us your tenant, not an intermediary. Your income is contractually guaranteed regardless of what happens with individual occupants.
What happens if a resident damages the property?
We are responsible for the property under the terms of our commercial lease. We carry out regular inspections and maintain the property to a high standard. Any damage is our liability, not yours.
Is the rent below market rate?
In exchange for guaranteed payment, zero voids, and zero management, rent is typically agreed slightly below full open market rate. Most landlords find the peace of mind more than compensates for the difference.
How does the Renters' Rights Act affect this arrangement?
It doesn't. The new legislation applies to assured tenancy agreements between landlords and individual tenants. A commercial lease with a CIC operates under a completely different legal framework — giving you the stability the new residential rules have removed.
What types of properties do you look for?
We're interested in shared houses (HMOs), self-contained flats, and bedsits in reasonable condition within our operating area. Get in touch and we'll discuss whether your property is a good fit.

Ready to talk about your property?

A free, no-obligation assessment. We'll tell you honestly whether your property works for us.
