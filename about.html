About Us

A business built
on belief.

Langford Housing CIC exists because the people who need support most deserve more than a system that processes them.

Our story

We started Langford because
it needed to exist.

Too often, the people who need support most are treated like a case number, not a person. We built Langford Housing CIC to change that.

We provide safe, supported accommodation for adults rebuilding their lives — with a dedicated support worker in their corner from day one. Not a rotating list of strangers. One person. Consistently.

As a Community Interest Company, we are legally required to act in the public interest. Every penny of surplus goes back into the homes we provide and the people we support. That's not a marketing line — it's written into who we are.

BM
Bilal Mohammad
Founder & Director, Langford Housing CIC

Legal structure

Why a CIC matters.

Community Interest Company

A CIC is a legal structure that locks our social purpose in place. It means we cannot be converted for private gain. Any profits must be reinvested into the community we serve. When you work with Langford Housing CIC, you're working with an organisation legally accountable to the people it helps — not to shareholders.
