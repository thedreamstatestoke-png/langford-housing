Accommodation and support that works together.

From emergency placements to self-contained flats, backed by dedicated support workers, benefits help, and a clear path towards independent living.

Accommodation

Types of accommodation we provide.

We match each person to the right setting for where they are now — and where they're heading.

01

Shared houses & HMOs

Well-managed shared homes where residents have their own room alongside the support of a household and staff on hand. A good fit for people who benefit from not being alone.

02

Self-contained bedsits

Your own private space with the reassurance of support nearby — a strong step towards greater independence.

03

Self-contained flats

Fully self-contained flats for residents ready to live independently, with support still only a phone call away.

04

Emergency & crisis accommodation

Safe accommodation for people in urgent housing need — because a crisis doesn't wait for a waiting list.

"Not just a roof — a room that feels like the start of something."

Support services

Support that doesn't stop at the front door.

Every resident has a dedicated support worker and a personalised plan. Here's what that support includes.

  • Dedicated support worker — one consistent person in your corner from day one.
  • Personalised support plans — built around your goals, not a template.
  • Benefits & Housing Benefit assistance — help claiming everything you're entitled to.
  • Mental health support — trauma-informed, and linked to local services.
  • Addiction recovery support — alongside you, without judgement.
  • Life skills development — cooking, budgeting, managing a tenancy.
  • Employment support — help getting into training, volunteering or work.
  • Move-on & independent living — a clear plan and support to make the step.
