What We Offer

Safe homes.
Real support.
A clear path forward.

More than a place to stay — every person we house gets a dedicated support worker and a plan built around them.

Our services

01
Safe Accommodation
Shared houses, self-contained bedsits, and move-on flats — a clear pathway from crisis to independence. All our homes are clean, secure, and genuinely yours for as long as you need them.
02
A Dedicated Support Worker
A real person who sits down with you, understands your situation, and works alongside you toward your goals. Not a rotating list of strangers — one person, consistently, from day one.
03
Benefits & Practical Help
Housing benefit applications, universal credit, medical appointments, registering with a GP — we help you sort the things that feel impossible when you're in crisis.
04
Wellbeing & Recovery Support
Mental health signposting, addiction recovery support, and a trusted referral network. We connect you to the right specialist help at the right time.
05
24/7 Emergency Cover
Life doesn't only happen between 9 and 5. Our on-call support means there's always someone to reach when something goes wrong. You're never facing a crisis alone.
06
Move-On to Independence
The goal is always forward. When you're ready to step into independent living, we help you make that transition — and we don't disappear the moment you do.

The journey

What moving in actually looks like.

For new residents, the first few weeks can feel overwhelming. Here's what to expect from us.

1
You arrive. We make sure the home is ready, the basics are sorted, and you know who to call if you need anything.
2
You meet your support worker. They take time to understand your situation — your goals, your worries, your pace.
3
Together, you build a support plan — a real conversation about what you need and where you want to go.
4
Regular check-ins, practical help, and honest conversations as things change. We adapt alongside you.
5
When you're ready, we support your move to independent living — and stay in touch until you're settled.
