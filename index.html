( Our purpose )

We exist for people who've been let down or turned away — those experiencing homelessness, and adults living with mental health needs or working through addiction recovery. Everyone deserves a safe place to live and someone in their corner. We provide both, and we don't stop at the front door.

How can we help?

We work with the people we house — and the people who refer them.

What we provide

Accommodation and support, together.

A home is only the start. Every resident gets a personalised support plan and a dedicated worker who stays alongside them.

01

Shared houses & HMOs

Well-managed shared homes where residents have their own space alongside the support of a household and on-hand staff.

02

Self-contained flats & bedsits

For those ready for more independence — private, self-contained accommodation with support still only a call away.

03

Emergency & crisis accommodation

When someone needs a safe place tonight, not next month. Rapid placements for people in urgent housing need.

04

Wrap-around support

Mental health support, addiction recovery, benefits help, life skills, employment support and a clear move-on plan.

Why Langford Housing

People first. Always.

We work with the person, not just the paperwork. Our team is trained in trauma-informed care, we don't disappear after move-in day, and we keep referring agencies informed at every step.

Based at Merrydale House in Bradford, we're a local provider that answers within hours — not weeks.

For referring professionals

Refer with confidence. We'll keep you in the loop.

When you refer a client to Langford Housing, you're connecting them with a team that communicates clearly and works alongside you throughout the placement.

1

Submit a referral

Complete our online referral form or get in touch directly. We acknowledge every referral quickly.

2

Assessment & matching

We assess needs and match your client to the right accommodation and the right level of support.

3

Placement & ongoing updates

Once placed, you're kept informed at every milestone for as long as it's needed.
