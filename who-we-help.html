Who We Help

Whatever brought
you here,

We support adults from all walks of life. No judgment about how you got here — only focus on where you're going next.

Who we support

Adults aged 18+
rebuilding their lives.

We don't define people by their circumstances. We see where they want to go and work alongside them to get there.

People experiencing homelessness Mental health needs Addiction recovery Care leavers & young adults Ex-offenders & prison leavers Domestic abuse survivors Learning disabilities Refugees & asylum seekers

How to access our services

Three ways in.
One outcome.

Agency Referral
Councils, NHS trusts, homelessness charities, and probation services can refer directly. We acknowledge every referral within 48 hours.
Self-Referral
If you're looking for housing yourself, you can get in touch directly. You don't need to go through an agency — just reach out and we'll talk it through.
Call Us Direct
Sometimes you just need to speak to someone. Call us directly and a real person will answer. No automated systems, no hold music.
