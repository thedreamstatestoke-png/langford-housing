The people we're here for.

We support adults aged 18 and over across Bradford — people experiencing homelessness, and those living with mental health needs or working through addiction recovery.

Who we support

Everyone deserves a safe place to start again.

01

People experiencing homelessness

Whether someone is rough sleeping, sofa-surfing, or at imminent risk of losing their home, we offer safe accommodation and a way forward — quickly, and without judgement.

02

Adults with mental health needs

Our trauma-informed team provides stable housing and support that works alongside mental health services, helping people feel secure enough to move forward.

03

Adults in addiction recovery

A stable, supportive environment matters in recovery. We stand alongside people working through addiction, connecting them to the right support at the right time.

04

Adults aged 18 and over

We support adults of all ages from 18 upwards. Every person is assessed individually and matched to the accommodation and support that fits them.

How people reach us

Access is through referral.

To make sure people get the right support, we take referrals from professionals who know the person's situation:

1

Local homelessness agencies

Outreach teams and homelessness charities can refer directly.

2

Local authority & council

We work with council housing and social care teams across Bradford.
