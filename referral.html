Home / Make a Referral

Refer someone to a safe home.

Our online referral form is for professionals — homelessness agencies, local authorities and councils. Tell us about the person you're supporting and we'll respond quickly.

Who can refer

Referrals from professionals.

To make sure people get the right support, we accept referrals from professionals who know the person's circumstances:

1

Homelessness agencies

Outreach teams and homelessness charities supporting someone who needs accommodation.

2

Local authority & council

Housing and social care teams placing adults into supported accommodation.

3

What happens next

We acknowledge your referral, assess needs, and match your client to the right accommodation and support — keeping you informed throughout.

Need to talk it through first?

If a case is urgent or complex, email us and we'll get back to you quickly.
