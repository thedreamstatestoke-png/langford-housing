The referral process

Simple. Fast.
Transparent throughout.

1
Send the referral
Use the form on our Contact page or call us direct. Share what you can — we'll ask for anything else we need.
2
We acknowledge within 48 hours
Every referral acknowledged within 48 hours. You'll have a named contact from the very first message — no chasing required.
3
We assess together — honestly
We talk through your client's needs, our current capacity, and whether we're the right fit. If we're not, we'll say so clearly.
4
They move in, supported from day one
Your client arrives to a safe home and a dedicated support worker. You stay informed throughout.
5
Ongoing updates as needed
We provide updates at agreed intervals and proactively flag anything significant. Your client's wellbeing is a shared responsibility.
